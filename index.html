Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 06/23/2025

At Swipe to Clean, your privacy is extremely important to us. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Local Photos
Swipe to Clean accesses your photo library only on your device to help you organize and clean up unwanted images. We do not collect, upload, or store your photos or personal content in any way.

Usage Data
We do not track your activity or usage data. No analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs are used in this app.

2. How Your Data Is Used

Your data remains entirely on your device. We use access to your photo library solely to display images for cleaning and organizing purposes. All processing happens locally on your iPhone.

3. In-App Purchases

Swipe to Clean offers optional in-app subscriptions to unlock premium features. Purchases are processed through Apple’s secure App Store system. We do not receive or store any payment information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any personal data with third parties. We do not use or store any user data for advertising, analytics, or marketing.

5. Your Rights

Because your data never leaves your device, there is no account to delete and no personal data stored by us. You can revoke the app's photo access at any time in your iPhone Settings.
